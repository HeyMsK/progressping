# 💬 GradeRelay

**Parent Progress Report Generator for Teachers — Pre-K through 12th Grade**

GradeRelay is a fast, FERPA-compliant web app that helps teachers generate warm, professional progress reports for parents and guardians — in seconds. Select from hundreds of feedback prompts across general categories and subject-specific areas, add a personal note, and instantly copy or export a polished, ready-to-send report.

🌐 **Live at [graderelay.com](https://graderelay.com)**

---

## ✨ Features

- **Hundreds of feedback prompts** across 10 general categories and 12 subject-specific areas
- **FERPA-compliant by design** — no student names collected or stored. Reports address "your child" throughout
- **Optional fill-in blank** — toggle to add `_______________` for handwriting the name after printing
- **Three report types:** Daily · Weekly · Marking Period
- **Marking Period selector** — choose from Marking Period, Quarter, Trimester, Semester, Term, Mid-Year, End of Year, or Custom
- **Student grade level** — optional Pre-K through 12th Grade selector
- **Subject-Specific Prompts** — 12 subject areas with targeted strength and growth language:
  - 🌱 Pre-K & Kindergarten · 📚 ELA · 🔢 Math · 🔬 Science · 🌍 Social Studies
  - 🎨 The Arts · 🏃 Physical Education · 💚 SEL · 🎓 AP · 🌐 ENL · 💡 Special Education · 🔵 General
- **Five export options:** Copy Text · Send via Email · Send via Text/SMS · View Formatted Report · Download .doc
- **PWA** — installable on iPhone and Android, works offline
- **No login required** — works instantly in any browser
- **No data stored** — nothing saved to any server

## 🔒 Privacy & FERPA Compliance

GradeRelay is built from the ground up for student privacy:

- **No student names are collected** — reports address "your child" throughout
- **No data is transmitted to any server** — everything runs locally in the browser
- **No login, no accounts, no tracking**
- Optional fill-in blank toggle lets teachers handwrite names after printing — keeping digital records name-free
- Teachers are responsible for sending reports through FERPA-compliant platforms

> ⚠️ GradeRelay does not store, transmit, or retain any student or parent information. It is the teacher's responsibility to comply with their school or district's data privacy policies when sending generated reports.

## 🚀 Getting Started

GradeRelay is a single-file React app — no build step required.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/HeyMsK/graderelay.git
cd graderelay
```

Open `index.html` in any browser, or visit **[graderelay.com](https://graderelay.com)**.
